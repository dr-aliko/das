Notes · system reuse + decisions

01 · Reuses

Existing DAS tokens

  • · App bar + logo (Phase 2 chrome)
  • .das-card · all topic/month/warning cards
  • ink-* palette · 100% reuse, no new colors
  • Subject-color left-border accents (existing)
  • Steel-blue primary, amber warn, ruby danger, emerald success — unchanged
  • Tabular nums on hours/dates/counts
  • Bottom sheet drag handle pattern (Phase 5)
  • Filter chip pattern (Phase 3)
02 · Decisions

Why these specifics

  • · 3-col workspace: pool/board/sidebar is the canonical kanban-editor IA. 6/3/3 split keeps the board (working area) widest.
  • · Months 2×3 grid: 5 AYT months fit cleanly in 2 columns. Horizontal scroll only needed if > 6 months. The 5th month spans both columns.
  • · Warnings as advisory: amber/ink left-borders distinguish severity; no blocking modals. Coach decides.
  • · Mobile = tabs + sheet: drag-and-drop dies on phones. Replaced with "+ Aya Ekle" → bottom sheet picker.
  • · Halledilmiş zone: line-through + opacity makes it feel "removed" without losing the card. Restore is one tap.
